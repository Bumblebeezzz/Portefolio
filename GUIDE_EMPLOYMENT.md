# Work & clients — using this portfolio

## Transparency

**No job or client is “guaranteed.”** Market conditions and timing depend on your niche, the economy, and your outreach. This guide describes what **increases your odds** fastest.

## Four-layer strategy

1. **Warm network (priority)** — 20–30 personalized messages: past clients, colleagues, founder communities. Often higher response rates than cold.
2. **Portfolio link** — One line + hosted portfolio URL in email signature and LinkedIn.
3. **Marketplaces** — Upwork, Malt, etc.: respond quickly to “automation”, “n8n”, “lead gen” posts.
4. **Niche** — Pick a vertical (e.g. agencies, solar, professional firms) and cite **Lead Gen Tax** as proof of real lead gen work.

## Hosting the portfolio URL

- **Netlify / Vercel** — Deploy this repo (static).
- **GitHub Pages** — `gh-pages` branch or `/docs` folder.
- **Flask** — `pip install -r requirements.txt` then `flask run` (see README).
